# Payments FAQs

## What payment methods are accepted?
- Credit & debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- PayPal
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Gift cards & store credit

## Is it safe to enter my card details?
Yes. All payments are processed by Adyen and protected by 3D Secure (SCA). StyleCo never stores your full card number.

## When is my card charged?
For card payments, your card is authorized at checkout and charged when the order ships. For PayPal and Klarna, the provider's terms apply.

## Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons: insufficient funds, expired card, billing address mismatch, or your bank flagging the transaction. Try a different payment method or contact your bank — StyleCo doesn't see the decline reason.

## Can I split payment across two methods?
Not at checkout, but you can apply a gift card or store credit and pay the remainder with another method.

## Do you offer financing?
Klarna's "Pay in 3" splits your order into three interest-free installments. Available on orders €35–€1000 in most EU countries.
