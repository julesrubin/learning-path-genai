# General FAQs

## Where are StyleCo's stores?
StyleCo operates 200+ stores across 12 European countries: France, Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Germany, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Austria, Sweden, Denmark, and Finland. Use the **Store Locator** on our website to find your nearest store.

## What are typical store opening hours?
Most stores open Monday–Saturday 10:00–20:00 and Sunday 11:00–19:00. Hours vary by location and country (Sunday closures apply in Germany and Austria). Check the Store Locator for exact hours.

## Can I reserve an item in store?
Yes. From any product page, click **Reserve in Store**, pick your store, and we'll hold the item for 48 hours. You'll get a confirmation SMS once it's ready.

## Do you offer gift cards?
StyleCo gift cards are available in denominations from €25 to €500, redeemable online and in-store across all 12 countries. They're valid for 24 months from purchase.

## Is there a loyalty program?
Yes — **StyleCo Plus** is free to join. You earn 1 point per €1 spent; 200 points = €10 voucher. Members also get free express shipping and early access to sales.
