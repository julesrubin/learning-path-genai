# Account FAQs

## Do I need an account to order?
No — guest checkout is available. But an account lets you track orders, save addresses, manage returns, and join StyleCo Plus.

## I forgot my password — what do I do?
On the login page, click **Forgot password**. Enter your email and you'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires in 1 hour.

## How do I delete my account?
Go to **Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account**. Deletion is permanent and processes within 30 days (EU GDPR). Open orders must be completed or cancelled first.

## Can I change the email address on my account?
Yes — go to **Account Settings → Profile**. You'll need to confirm the change via a link sent to the new email.

## Where do I update my address book?
**Account Settings → Addresses**. You can store up to 10 addresses and mark one as default for shipping and another as default for billing.

## How do I unsubscribe from marketing emails?
Click **Unsubscribe** at the bottom of any email, or toggle off email marketing in **Account Settings → Communications**. Order confirmations and shipping updates will still be sent.
