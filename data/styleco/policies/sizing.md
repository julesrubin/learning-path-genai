## Women's Bottoms
StyleCo bottoms run **true to size**. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing **down** for stretch fabrics (denim with elastane, jersey) and **up** for non-stretch (linen, structured wool).

## Footwear
Sold in EU sizing only. We recommend ordering your usual EU size — our footwear is built on a standard last with no half-size run.

## Fit Guidance
Each product page shows a **Fit indicator** (Slim / Regular / Relaxed) and the model's height + size for reference. When in doubt, size up — returns are free in the EU.
