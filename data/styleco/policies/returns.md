# StyleCo Returns & Exchanges Policy

## Return Window
You may return any item within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund to the original payment method. Sale items marked "Final Sale" are not returnable.

## Condition Requirements
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.
- Underwear, swimwear, and pierced jewelry cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.
- Footwear must be returned in the original box, unmarked.

## How to Return
1. Log into your StyleCo account and open the order under **My Orders**.
2. Click **Request Return** and select the items + reason.
3. Print the prepaid return label (free in EU; €4.95 deducted from refund elsewhere).
4. Drop the package at any partner pickup point within 14 days.

## Exchanges
Exchanges are processed as a return + new order. We cannot hold stock during the exchange — if your size sells out, you'll receive a refund instead.

## Refund Timing
Refunds appear on your statement within **5–10 business days** after we receive the return. You'll get an email when the refund is issued.

## Defective Items
Items that arrive defective or damaged are eligible for a full refund **plus** the original shipping cost. Contact support@styleco.eu within 7 days with photos.
