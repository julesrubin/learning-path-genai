# StyleCo Shipping Policy

## Delivery Times (EU)
| Country | Standard | Express |
|---------|----------|---------|
| France, Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg | 2–3 business days | Next business day |
| Germany, Italy, Spain | 3–5 business days | 1–2 business days |
| Other EU | 5–7 business days | 2–3 business days |

## Shipping Costs
- **Free standard shipping** on orders over €50 within the EU.
- Standard shipping below €50: €4.95.
- Express shipping: €9.95 flat rate.

## Outside the EU
StyleCo ships to the UK, Switzerland, and Norway. Customs duties are calculated at checkout (DDP — no surprises at delivery). Delivery: 5–10 business days.

## Order Cutoff
Orders placed before **14:00 CET** ship the same business day. Orders placed after the cutoff or on weekends ship on the next business day.

## Tracking
You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as the parcel leaves our warehouse. Tracking updates every 4–6 hours.

## Missed Deliveries
If you're not home, the carrier will leave a notice and attempt redelivery once. After that, the parcel goes to the nearest pickup point and is held for 7 days before being returned to us.
